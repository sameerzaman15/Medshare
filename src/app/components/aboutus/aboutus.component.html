Welcome to MedWay!

"MEDWAY" is the pioneering medical-hailing service in Pakistan, addressing critical transportation issues for timely access to clinics and hospitals. By combining medical services with transportation, MEDWAY offers an affordable and efficient solution for emergencies. Its dual role as a business and service provider enhances its unique value proposition. MEDWAY's equipped vans ensure patients receive immediate care en route to medical facilities. The slogan, #SaveTimeSaveLives, encapsulates its mission to save both time and lives.

Who We Are?

We are MEDWAY, the first medical-hailing service in Pakistan, revolutionizing emergency medical transportation. Our mission is to provide timely and affordable access to healthcare facilities for everyone, especially in urgent situations. Equipped with medical equipment and trained personnel, our vans ensure immediate care during transit. We bridge the gap between critical medical needs and accessibility, striving to save both time and lives. Our unique value proposition makes us a trusted partner in healthcare and emergency response.

Our Mission

Our mission at MEDWAY is to revolutionize emergency medical transportation in Pakistan by providing timely, affordable, and reliable access to healthcare facilities. We aim to bridge the gap between urgent medical needs and accessibility, ensuring immediate care with our equipped vans and trained personnel. By saving crucial time in emergencies, we strive to save lives and enhance the overall health and well-being of our community.
